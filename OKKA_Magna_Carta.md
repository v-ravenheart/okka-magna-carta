## 03 · Strategy

*Where the organism is going. The guiding intelligence behind all decisions.*

### The Guiding Question
**How do we build intelligence systems that increase human agency and sovereignty while reducing cognitive and executive burden instead of adding to it?**

### The Founding Thesis
Three simultaneous crises created the conditions for OKKA:

**Crisis 1 — The Post-Job Economy**
The specific institutional container through which effort has been organised, compensated, and given social meaning for 200 years is dissolving. The one-person company is the emerging default unit of economic participation. People are building alone, without the infrastructure that teams provided.

**Crisis 2 — The AI Literacy Gap**
AI tools are being deployed at scale without the human infrastructure to use them. The gap between the promise of AI and its operational reality is producing frustration, fragmentation, and wasted effort. Most people interact with AI as a chat window, not as operational infrastructure. The capability exists. The literacy does not.

**Crisis 3 — Cognitive Overload and the Absence of Relief**
