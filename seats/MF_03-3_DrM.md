## 04 · THE ACADEMIC ROAD STRATEGY

The academic road is not a third branch. It is the credibility root system.

The argument: research credibility becomes grant eligibility becomes investor signal. The academic track feeds the other three branches — OÜ (IP provenance via DOIs), Ventura (investor credibility via publications and institutional affiliations), and House of Us (research legitimacy for ASBL status and social economy funding).

Without the academic road:
- IP claims have no independent timestamp → Zenodo solves this
- Grant applications have no research pedigree → publications and affiliations solve this
- Investor conversations lack institutional credibility → a PhD programme and conference presence solve this

The academic road is slow by design. It does not pivot. It does not sprint. It publishes, presents, and builds the intellectual foundation that makes everything else credible.
